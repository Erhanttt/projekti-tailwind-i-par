Cube
Restaurant

Surrounded by glass on all sides and offering fantastic views of Stuttgart city center, the CUBE Restaurant in the KUNSTMUSEUM STUTTGART art gallery is a unique, atmospheric setting. The understated interior styled by acclaimed designer Heinz Witthöft adds to the impression of urban expanse. The professional restaurant team has a clear vision: “Deliver a new dining experience!” This superb location deserves no less.

HOURS

BOOK
ONLINE

Welcome to the CUBE Restaurant. You can use our easy online booking system to reserve a table for up to fourteen people. Online bookings must be made at least 24 hours in advance. Please give us a call if you would like to make a last-minute booking for the same day and we’d be happy to help.

Unfortunately, window seats cannot be guaranteed. If it is no longer possible to book a table online or if you would like to make a booking for more than five people, please don’t hesitate to call us on +49 (0)711 / 93964279 or to send us an email.

LUNCH

Light, fresh, continually updated, and inspired by regional and international cuisine, our menu delights business guests looking for a quick lunch and leisurely art lovers alike. In the afternoon, freshly baked cakes tempt you to take a coffee break.

FROM 8 PERSONS

For groups of 8-19 people, we kindly request that you choose one of our lunch menus.

DINNER

The cuisine served in the CUBE Restaurant is contemporary and cutting-edge. In fact, we call it “inspired cooking”.

Quintessentially inspiring, it takes diners on a voyage of culinary discovery. Using top-quality and, wherever possible, regional ingredients, we create exceptionally delicious dishes with clear origins. Mediterranean and Asian influences are combined with fresh takes on elements of traditional cuisine. The result is irresistible!

FROM 8 PERSONS

For groups of 8-19 people, we kindly request that you choose one of our evening menus.

COFFEE &
CAKE

Creamy, fruity, fluffy, and light – from cheesecake and raspberry gateau to apple pie, we have sweet treats to suit everyone’s taste. Indulge in our cakes and pies – freshly baked every day.
